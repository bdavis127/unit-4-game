Crystals Collector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number displayed. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden and can only be determined by verifying how much each crystal is adding to your current score once you click on it.

Each time a new game starts, the random number will change and so will the values of the crystal.
